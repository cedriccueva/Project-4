Pulang Araw

Colonel Yuta Saitoh stands as a figure of strategic brilliance and formidable presence. Known for his sharp intellect and mastery in warfare, he is a leader whose every move is calculated and deliberate. His ability to command the battlefield with precision is unmatched, and his words, like weapons, hold the power to influence both allies and adversaries alike.

In the world of conflict and loyalty, Saitoh has proven himself as a master of both tactics and psychology. His influence extends beyond the confines of the battlefield, as he manipulates the delicate balance between trust and betrayal. His unwavering determination and ability to remain unseen in the shadows make him a formidable force, ensuring his success in the most challenging of situations.

"I walk down the road, paving it with shattered and shredded dreams. I am a man of capability, a shadow in the night."
